Who we are?

We are a community of Minecraft players who love to challenge ourselves and survive in a harsh world. Our server features a custom lifesteal plugin that makes every fight a matter of life and death. You can heal yourself by damaging your enemies, but they can do the same to you. This adds a new layer of strategy and excitement to the game. Whether you want to explore, build, or battle, you will find something to enjoy on our server. Join us today and see if you have what it takes to survive!

Survival World

Welcome to the Survival world, where every hit matters. In this world, you can steal health from other players and mobs by Killing them, but you also lose health when you get Killed. You have to balance your aggression and defense, and use your resources wisely. The Lifesteal SMP world is a challenging and exciting place to explore, build, and fight.
